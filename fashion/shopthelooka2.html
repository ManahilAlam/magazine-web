Statement Sleeves – The Bold Trend Making Waves in Fashion

Submitted by: Mahrez Staff

Sleeves are stealing the spotlight this season — bigger, bolder, and more dramatic than ever. From puffs to bells, statement sleeves are the go-to trend for fashionistas across Pakistan.

The Power of the Sleeve

Statement sleeves instantly elevate your outfit. A plain kurti becomes catwalk-ready with voluminous sleeves. Whether it’s a casual outing or a festive event, let your sleeves do the talking.

Designers are loving exaggerated silhouettes this year — think puffs, flares, and layered ruffles, all tailored in trendy fabrics like cotton, lawn, and chiffon.

Types of Statement Sleeves to Try

  • Puff Sleeves – Vintage and feminine
  • Bell Sleeves – Flowy and dramatic
  • Bishop Sleeves – Elegant and loose-fitted
  • Ruffled Sleeves – Fun, layered detailing
  • Structured Sleeves – Bold cuts and power shapes
