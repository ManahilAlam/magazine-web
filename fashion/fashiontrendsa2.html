Denim on Denim: How to Nail the Look

Submitted by: Mahrez Staff

Double denim is no longer a fashion faux pas — it’s a statement. Master the art of pairing your favorite denim pieces without looking overdone.

The Comeback of Denim on Denim

Denim has evolved from utility wear to an icon of everyday style. The denim-on-denim trend is now embraced by celebrities, influencers, and fashion-forward youth across Pakistan. The trick? Balance and boldness.

You can now find structured denim kurtis, belted denim tops, and wide-leg jeans with matching jackets — all with flair.

How to Style Denim on Denim

  • Mix light and dark washes to add contrast
  • Break it up with a neutral belt or solid top
  • Layer with minimal jewelry — let denim do the talking

Whether you’re dressing up or keeping it casual, double denim is a sure way to stand out. Make it your signature, and let your denim do the talking.
