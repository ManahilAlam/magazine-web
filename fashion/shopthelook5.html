Embellished Dupattas – The Glamour Upgrade You Need

Submitted by: Mahrez Staff

Embellished dupattas are no longer just add-ons — they’re the statement-makers of Pakistani fashion, instantly elevating any simple outfit into festive glam.

Why Embellished Dupattas Are Trending

With mirror work, sequins, thread embroidery, and gotta detailing, embellished dupattas add instant charm to even the plainest of suits or kurtas. They’re budget-friendly, versatile, and very *desi chic*.

Designers and local brands are offering ready-to-wear embellished dupattas that can mix and match with multiple outfits, making them a must-have wardrobe essential.

How to Style Embellished Dupattas

  • Pair a mirror-work dupatta with a plain black lawn kurta
  • Drape a sequined dupatta with a basic black shalwar kameez for a wedding look
  • Use a net embroidered dupatta with your maxi or lehenga for Mehndi vibes

With the right embellished dupatta, you don’t need a new outfit—just the right sparkle. Embrace the glam, desi-style.
