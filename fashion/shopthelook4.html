Maxi Dresses with Desi Prints

Submitted by: Mahrez Staff

Flowy, modest, and beautifully printed — maxi dresses with desi motifs are taking over Pakistani fashion. They blend culture with comfort like never before.

Why Desi-Printed Maxis Are Trending

Desi motifs like paisleys, block prints, ajrak, and floral butis have made a stunning comeback. Combined with the grace of maxi silhouettes, they create outfits that are breezy and beautiful.

Designers across Pakistan are reimagining maxis in cotton, lawn, and chiffon — perfect for hot summers or wedding festivities.

How to Style Your Desi Maxi

  • Add a statement jhumka or choker for a traditional touch
  • Pair with khussas or kolhapuri sandals
  • Layer with a waistcoat or cropped shrug for semi-formal occasions

Maxi dresses with desi flair are timeless, versatile, and effortlessly chic. Every print tells a story — and now, it can be your story.
