5 Natural Desi Face Packs That Actually Work!

Submitted by: Mahrez Staff

In a world full of expensive skincare products, sometimes the best solutions are already sitting in your kitchen. Today, we’re diving into 5 timeless desi face packs that our moms, nanis, and dadis have been using for generations — because they actually work.

1. Haldi (Turmeric) and Yogurt Face Pack

Haldi is antibacterial, and yogurt soothes and moisturizes.

How to make:

  • 1 teaspoon haldi
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt

Mix into a paste and apply for 15 minutes.

Best for: Acne-prone or dull skin.

2. Besan (Gram Flour) and Rose Water Pack

Besan gently exfoliates, while rose water refreshes the skin.

How to make:

  • 2 tablespoons besan
  • Enough rose water to make a smooth paste

Apply evenly and wash off after 20 minutes.

Best for: Oily skin and tan removal.

3. Multani Mitti (Fuller's Earth) and Cucumber Juice

Multani mitti cools and tightens pores, cucumber hydrates.

How to make:

  • 2 tablespoons multani mitti
  • 3 tablespoons cucumber juice

Apply and let dry before rinsing off.

Best for: Hot summer days, large pores.

4. Aloe Vera and Honey Glow Pack

Aloe heals irritated skin, and honey adds a natural glow.

How to make:

  • 2 tablespoons fresh aloe vera gel
  • 1 tablespoon raw honey

Mix well, leave on for 20 minutes.

Best for: Sensitive and dry skin.

5. Sandalwood Powder and Milk Pack

Sandalwood soothes redness and improves complexion, milk softens skin.

How to make:

  • 1 tablespoon sandalwood powder
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Form a paste and apply for 15–20 minutes.

Best for: Brightening and calming the skin.

You don’t always need fancy products when nature’s kitchen has your back.
