2. Besan (Gram Flour) and Rose Water Pack

Besan gently exfoliates, while rose water refreshes the skin.

  • 2 tablespoons besan

Apply evenly and wash off after 20 minutes.

Best for: Oily skin and tan removal.

3. Multani Mitti (Fuller's Earth) and Cucumber Juice

Multani mitti cools and tightens pores, cucumber hydrates.

How to make:

  • 2 tablespoons multani mitti
  • 3 tablespoons cucumber juice

Apply and let dry before rinsing off.

Best for: Hot summer days, large pores.

4. Aloe Vera and Honey Glow Pack

Aloe heals irritated skin, and honey adds a natural glow.

How to make:

  • 1 tablespoon raw honey

Mix well, leave on for 20 minutes.

Best for: Sensitive and dry skin.
